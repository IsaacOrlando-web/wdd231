Site Name: ChileReady

The name instantly communicates the website’s purpose: helping travelers get ready for their trip to Chile by learning key phrases and travel tips.

Purpose: ChileReady provides tourists with quick-access Spanish phrases (with audio) and key travel info—from transport tips to top attractions—all in one simple, interactive guide.

Scenarios:

  • I’ve never been to Chile and don’t speak Spanish well. What basic phrases should I learn before my trip, and how do I pronounce them correctly?
  • I’m already in Chile and struggling to ask for directions to Cerro San Cristóbal in Santiago. How do I say this in Spanish, and what’s the best way to get there?
  • What are the top historic sites in Valparaíso, and how do I ask about entry fees in Spanish?
